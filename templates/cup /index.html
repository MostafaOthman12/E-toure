Welcome in your second home


If you’re looking for history, culture and an experience like no other, then touch-down in Cairo to explore the Pyramids, the Sphinx, and other remarkable century-old wonders of the world. Or maybe you can start further south in the historic temples of Luxor and Abu Simblel, go hot air ballooning over the Valley of the Kings, take a felukka ride in Aswan and camp with a people who ‘invented’ hospitality. And whenever you’re ready, be sure to hit up any of the world class Red sea resort destinations where snorkeling, diving, kite surfing, yacht riding, off-road safaris, and youth-replenishing beach relaxation await, not to mention a booming night-life scene.
